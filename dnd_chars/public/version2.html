Features & Feats

  • Divine Smite: Spend a spell slot to deal extra radiant damage on a melee hit.
  • Lay on Hands: Heal hit points equal to Paladin level × 5.

  • Aura of Courage: Allies within 10 ft. can’t be frightened.
  • Hunter’s Mark: Bonus action to mark an enemy, adding +1d6 damage per hit.
  • Misty Step: Teleport 30 ft as a bonus action.
  • Lucky (Feat): Reroll 3 dice per long rest.
  • Dueling Fighting Style: When wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons, gain +2 to damage rolls.
  • Channel Divinity - Abjure Enemy: Frighten one enemy within 60 ft. (Wisdom save) for 1 minute; fiends/undead have disadvantage.
  • Channel Divinity - Vow of Enmity: Bonus action to gain advantage on attack rolls against one creature for 1 minute.
